# Motor Driver Specifications

## Electrical Parameters

The driver supports various electrical configurations for different motor types and applications.

### Input Voltage Range
- Minimum: 10V DC
- Maximum: 50V DC  
- Recommended: 24V DC for optimal performance

### Current Output Capabilities
- Continuous: 6A RMS
- Peak: 8A for short bursts
- Efficiency: >95% at rated load

## Control Features

Advanced control capabilities for precise motor operation with multiple feedback options.

### Microstepping Resolution
- Range: 1/1 to 1/256 steps per full step
- Accuracy: ±0.1% positioning error
- Smoothness: Optimized sine wave interpolation

### Communication Interfaces
- Step/Direction signals for CNC compatibility
- UART configuration interface
- CAN-FD networking for distributed systems

## Physical Specifications

Mechanical and environmental characteristics of the driver unit.

### Dimensions and Weight
- Size: 50mm × 40mm × 15mm compact form factor
- Weight: 45g lightweight design
- Mounting: DIN rail or screw mount options

### Environmental Ratings
- Operating Temperature: -20°C to +70°C
- Storage Temperature: -40°C to +85°C
- Humidity: 5% to 95% non-condensing

## Safety and Compliance

Regulatory compliance and safety features built into the driver.

### Protection Features
- Overcurrent protection with automatic recovery
- Thermal shutdown at 85°C junction temperature
- Reverse polarity protection on all inputs

### Certifications
- CE marking for European compliance
- FCC Part 15 Class B for EMI
- RoHS compliant materials
